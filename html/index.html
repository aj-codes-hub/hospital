Service oriented

We like to work together with you as a patient. Every patient is different and therefore a treatment is better suited for one than for another. Together we look at what is best for you. This is called shared decision making and for this it is important that we get to know you better!

Transparent

By using the WebApp you always have insight into your own medical data: view blood results and specialist letters, read what your GP has advised you. In addition, you can order repeat medication, schedule an online appointment or ask the GP for an e-mail consultation.

Short waiting times

We strive for short waiting times for an appointment. In case of a medical emergency, you can of course be seen the same day. The medical assistants are specially trained to assess the seriousness.
