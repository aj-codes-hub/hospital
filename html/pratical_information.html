Office hours

The practice is open all year round, except for holidays. Because we work with a team of permanent doctors, one of the permanent doctors will always be present.

Cancelling an appointment

If a previously made appointment cannot go ahead, please call the assistant as soon as possible. If you do not cancel the appointment in time (at least 24 hours in advance), costs may be charged for this.

The Practice of Internists

The Internists Practice is a small-scale independent practice that provides specialist medical care in the field of Internal Medicine. The patient is our central focus and we do this by giving personal attention to the patient with an eye for the person behind the medical complaints and symptom

The internist from De Praktijk Internisten holds consultations in your own GP practice, close by and familiar. This also makes it very easy to consult with your GP and practice assistant.

Internist D. Creemers has a biweekly consultation hour on Thursday morning at Praktijk Berestein. You can come here for all your health problems in the field of internism.

You can find more information about us on our
website:  www.depraktijkinternisten.nl

For questions about your appointment with the internist, you can call
070-2230038 or email  info@depraktijkinternisten.nl

Complaints Procedure

As a GP practice, we want to perform our work as professionally as possible and we believe it is important that you trust us. Of course, it may happen that you do not have a good feeling about the contact with our practice. For example, you may feel that you have not been taken seriously or that you have not been able to reach the practice properly. We would like to know about this as soon as possible, so that we can discuss this with you and learn from it. Youcan submit your complaint or comment by contacting one of our employees directly. One of our employees will then process your complaint as soon as possible.
